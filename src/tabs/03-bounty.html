Alignment

Revolutionary Rogue

Ajay Persaud operates outside the jurisdiction of any pirate crew, Marine command, or established faction. He is a revolutionary agent and independent operator — aligned loosely with the Revolutionary Army's ideology but beholden to no hierarchy. His philosophy is simple: systems that oppress are systems that must be rewritten.

He does not seek the One Piece. He does not seek a throne. What he seeks is the dismantling of the structures that use power to silence knowledge — and he builds his swarm as a living counter-argument to every weapon the World Government fields.

Known Accomplishments

The Silent Siege of Enies Lobby Annex — Infiltrated and systematically dismantled a secondary judicial fortress using only Worker and Pollen class insects over 72 hours. No alarm was raised until the building was functionally emptied of classified documents.
Liberation of the Wano Undersea Vault — Freed 340 political prisoners from a subterranean World Government holding facility beneath Wano's coastline. Used Chrysalis-class pressure-adapted insects to breach reinforced sea-stone doors from the inside.
Defeat of Vice Admiral Ashworth "Iron Tide" — Engaged and overwhelmed a senior Vice Admiral in a prolonged attrition battle. The swarm's adaptive evolution countered Ashworth's iron-manipulation abilities within three exchanges, producing rust-catalyzing enzyme-carriers in real time.
The Cipher Pol-7 Blackout — Single-handedly neutralized a Cipher Pol intelligence cell by deploying mirage and incense-class insects to destabilize their coordination, then used Glasswing scouts to extract their communications network before destroying the outpost.
The Sabaody Defiance — Publicly refused a Celestial Dragon's command to kneel. When the Dragon's escort attacked, Ajay deployed the Black Crown Protocol for the first recorded time, overwhelming an entire Marine escort platoon and two Rear Admirals in under ninety seconds.
Disruption of Buster Call Order 11-Gamma — Intercepted and scrambled the signal relay for a Buster Call deployment targeting a civilian island with Revolutionary sympathies. Used jellyfish-class Elemental insects to generate electromagnetic interference across the relay chain.
Assassination of Warden Galteus "The Gavel" — Eliminated the World Government's most feared prison warden during a coordinated Revolutionary strike on Impel Down's Level 3 transfer corridor. The kill was delivered by a single Venom-class insect, undetected until the warden collapsed mid-sentence.
