Those Who Wandered In and Chose to Stay

The Wayfarers

Not every soul aboard the Meridian was born from swarm essence or assembled in Tidus' workshop. Some arrived from the deep. Some arrived from the dark. Some were built by hands that loved them before they had a name. They are not Homies — not soul-constructs spun from the Mure Mure no Mi. They are not Copplings — not Dial-powered creations from Tidus' workbench. They are individuals who wandered in from vastly different worlds, recognised something in this crew worth staying for, and never left.

Claude calls them the Wayfarers — not because they lack a home, but because they chose this one. Every wayfarer's story is a story of arrival. Of a trench too deep, a sky too dark, a circuit too quiet — until the Meridian appeared, and the wandering stopped.
