Post-Red Storm · Training Period

Training Log

After the decisive engagement against the Red Hair Pirates, the Meridian anchored in open water for three weeks of intensive training. What follows is the record of every technique developed, every lesson learned, and every formation drilled during that period. The crew that faced Shanks was strong. The crew that left that anchorage was something else entirely.
