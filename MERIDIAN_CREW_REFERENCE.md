## Common Pronoun Traps

These are the characters most likely to have pronouns confused across sessions:

1. **Hinoko** — Female (she/her). Often confused because she idolizes Rime (male) and the Embers are chaotic/combative.
2. **Zenmai** — Female (she/her). Title "Foreman" can mislead. She has a winding key on her back.
3. **Yoru** — Female (she/her). Invisible in daylight. Night patrol. Can be mistaken for male due to stealth role.
4. **Copplings (Rivet, Sprocket, Gauge, Compass, Patch)** — All MALE (he/him). These five are frequently miswritten as female.
5. **Anvil** — FEMALE (she/her). Despite being "the heaviest hitter" and charging upside-down, Anvil is female.
6. **Claude** — NO gendered pronouns. Use "Claude" or "the spirit." Never he/she.
7. **Rime** — MALE (he/him). Previously had pronouns confused in Status Board. Always he/him.
