## Step 6: MAJOR REWRITE — Enlightened Frozen Guanyin of the Lotus Pool

### File: `src/tabs/08-combination-attacks.html` AND `src/tabs/panels/17-missions-16-17.html`

This is the signature full-crew ultimate attack. It needs significant reimagining.

### Old elemental contributions:
- Rime: Frozen Buddhas, absolute cold, ice
- Tidus: Coral reef, water
- Petal: Lotus pool, botanical
- Ajay: Bodhi/Enlightenment, swarm architecture
- Claude: Coordination, Mote network

### NEW elemental contributions:
- **Tidus**: NOW provides the ice/cold + coral. The frozen landscape, the temperature drop, the frozen constructs. His polar bear ice + shell shovel coral creates the foundation — a frozen reef world
- **Rime**: NOW provides Electro saturation across the battlefield + turns Tidus' ice/snow into frozen acid. His acidic vapor saturates the frozen landscape, making every ice surface chemically hostile (contact delivers cold damage AND chemical burns as it melts). The entire frozen world is electrified — lightning arcs between ice crystals. Rime also provides the dust that gives the frozen world a glittering, crystalline quality
- **Petal**: Lotus pool, botanical constructs, Dryad Forms. The lotuses now grow from Tidus' coral reef substrate, rooted in frozen coral
- **Ajay**: Bodhi/Enlightenment aura, swarm architecture, overall direction. The Buddha golems are now a collaborative creation — Tidus' ice forms the structure, Rime's acid-Electro animates them, Petal's botanical energy gives them the appearance of enlightened beings, and Ajay's Bodhi infuses them with sovereign will
- **Claude**: Twelve Modules coordination, Mote network sensing, tactical orchestration

### The reimagined Guanyin:
- Tidus slams his shell shovel into the ground. A frozen coral reef erupts outward — living coral formations encased in glittering ice, spreading across the battlefield. The temperature plummets. Snow begins to fall.
- Rime's acidic vapor floods the frozen landscape. Every ice surface becomes chemically hostile — beautiful but deadly. His Electro saturates the field, turning it into a crackling web of frozen lightning. Mineral dust gives everything a crystalline shimmer.
- Petal's botanical power takes root in Tidus' coral substrate. The lotus pool forms — frozen water with lotuses growing through the ice, their roots threading through living coral. Her Dryad constructs emerge from the frozen garden.
- Ajay's Bodhi/Enlightenment infuses the entire creation. The frozen Buddha golems form — collaborative constructs of ice (Tidus), acid-etched lightning (Rime), botanical grace (Petal), and sovereign will (Ajay). They are beautiful, terrifying, and enormous.
- Claude's Twelve Modules orchestrate the impossible logistics — each golem's position, each ice crystal's resonance, each acid pocket's pressure.
- The entire battlefield becomes a frozen, electrified, acid-laced, botanically-alive coral reef temple. The Guanyin sits at its center — a colossal Buddha of ice and coral and living wood, its hands folded in a mudra of absolute peace, its eyes glowing with the Golden Pulse.
- Shanks called on his entire crew to burn every drop of their combined Haki into a single desperate swing to shatter it. The Red Hair Pirates had nothing left afterward. The Meridian crew was still standing. Ajay chose mercy.
