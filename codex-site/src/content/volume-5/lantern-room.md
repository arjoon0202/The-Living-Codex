## The Lantern Room

Deep within the Meridian's Lower Deck sits a space unlike any other — the Lantern Room, the ship's most sacred sanctuary. It is where light lives. Where the crew's deepest hopes, most private sorrows, and most profound truths are held in the careful hands of someone who has never once broken a confidence.

This is not a room of walls and doors. It is a room of the heart — a place where the crew goes not to escape the world, but to remember why they are still here.
