## Named Techniques

## Signature Moves

Each technique is an entry in the Codex — a refined doctrine designed for a specific tactical purpose. The naming blends elegance with lethality: **beauty is strategy**.

---

### Cherry Blossom Fantasy
*Dream Dust Style*

**夢粉流・桜花幻想 — Yumegona-ryū: Ōka Gensō**
`Dream Dust` `Incense`

Pollen and Incense class insects disperse a shimmering, petal-like cloud of sleep dust and aromatic sedatives across the field. The particles catch light like falling cherry blossoms — achingly beautiful and impossibly gentle. Targets drift into a euphoric half-dream, aggression dissolving, movements softening, will to fight evaporating. By the time they realize the beauty was a weapon, they're already asleep.

---

### Forbidden Temptation
*Dream Dust Style*

**夢粉流・禁断の誘惑 — Yumegona-ryū: Kindan no Yūwaku**
`Pheromone` `Venom`

Pheromone class insects flood the area with an intoxicating cocktail that manipulates desire and attention. The target finds themselves irresistibly drawn toward a false opening the Codex has engineered. The temptation is the bait; the true strike comes from Venom class units positioned along the path the prey cannot help but walk. As the target commits, Ajay closes the distance with a Soru-burst and delivers a crescent kick wreathed in Soldier-class insects, driving them directly into the venom field. A trap the prey wants to enter, sealed by a kick they never see coming.

---

### Fabulous Fantasia
*Dream Dust Style*

**夢粉流・華麗幻想曲 — Yumegona-ryū: Karei Gensōkyoku**
`Dream Dust` `Pheromone` `Elemental`

The magnum opus of the Dream Dust style. Multiple castes deploy simultaneously — Pollen for shimmering visual distortion, Pheromone for emotional amplification, Incense for aromatic stupefaction, and Elemental jellyfish for drifting bioluminescent light. The result is total sensory overload rewriting the battlefield into a hallucinated wonderland. The target sees beauty, hears music in the buzzing, feels warmth — while the Codex quietly repositions every asset for a decisive strike. A symphony of deception orchestrated in real time.

### Somnabulist Cabeçada
*Dream Dust Style*

**夢粉流・夢遊頭突 — Yumegona-ryū: Muyū Zutsuki**
`Combat` `Dream Dust`

Dream Dust sedatives destabilize the opponent's balance while the Codex closes distance. The finishing blow is a brutal cabeçada — a headbutt — delivered with Herculean class insects compressed around the skull and forehead as living armor, backed by concentrated Armament Haki. A sleepwalker's headbutt that hits like a cannon. Often preceded by a feinting axe kick that scatters sleep pollen downward, forcing the target to inhale just as the headbutt arrives.

### Parfum Tae Wiang
*Aromatic Art*

**香術・タエ・ウィアン香 — Kōjutsu: Tae Wiang Kō**
`Incense` `Combat`

Named after the flowing, circular movements of traditional Thai dance. Incense class insects deploy in spiraling, ribbon-like formations trailing disorienting fragrance in sweeping arcs. The aesthetic is hypnotic — the swarm moves like a dancer's arms. Opponents caught in the spiral find spatial awareness collapsing. Meanwhile, Ajay moves within the spiral using Kami-e fluidity, delivering devastating roundhouse and spinning heel kicks from angles the perfumed haze has erased. Every kick trails incense. Every rotation deepens the disorientation. Movement as misdirection; fragrance as fog of war.

---

### Yophooliho-chagi Propolis
*Propolis Combat Art*

**蜂膠闘術・ヨプリョチャギ蜂膠 — Hōkō Tōjutsu: Yopuryochagi Hōkō**
`Combat` `Rokushiki`

A devastating kick technique fused with swarm engineering. Propolis — the resinous substance bees produce to reinforce their hives — is generated by Herculean class insects and compressed around the user's leg, forming a dense, amber-like armor shell. The kick is a yophooliho-chagi (spinning hook kick from Taekwondo), delivered with the weight of reinforced propolis and layered Armament Haki. On impact, the propolis shell shatters into adhesive fragments that pin and immobilize the target. Ajay frequently chains this from a Geppo air-step, descending with full rotational force. Power, style, and tactical aftereffect in a single strike.

---

### Hexagonal Compound Strike
*Compound Combat Art*

**六角複眼撃 — Rokkaku Fukugan Geki**
`Combat` `Rokushiki` `Sovereign`

Inspired by the compound eye of an insect — many lenses, one vision, total coverage. Ajay deploys six simultaneous attacks from six angles, each corresponding to a face of a hexagon. The sequence blends Rokushiki with swarm deployment: a Soru-propelled front kick launches the sequence, followed by Rankyaku slashes from two aerial Geppo positions, Soldier-class swarm strikes from left and right flanks, and a final Herculean-reinforced axe kick from directly above. All six impacts converge on the same target within a single heartbeat. The technique is the Codex's answer to opponents with perfect singular defense — because the attack is not singular. It is compound. Six vectors. One moment. No gaps.

### Dancing Centipede — Slashing Ruin
*Arthropod Combat Art*

**百足舞踏・斬滅 — Mukade Butō: Zanmetsu**
`Combat` `Rokushiki`

A relentless close-quarters combination inspired by the centipede — a hundred legs, all striking. Ajay enters a state of continuous rotational kicking, blending Rankyaku air slashes with physical strikes in a flowing chain that does not stop. Each kick launches a compressed air blade while Soldier-class insects trail every motion like a centipede's segmented body, each segment biting, cutting, and draining on contact. The rhythm accelerates with every rotation — low sweep into spinning back kick into rising heel into descending axe — all chained through Soru micro-bursts. It looks like a dance. It feels like being fed into a shredder. The technique ends only when the Codex decides the opponent has nothing left to defend with.

### Butterfly Breathing — Caprice
*Butterfly Art*

**蝶呼吸・気紛れ — Chō Kokyū: Kimagure**
`Incense` `Combat` `Pheromone`

The most unpredictable technique in the Codex. "Caprice" means whim — and that is exactly the philosophy. Ajay deploys a swirling cloak of butterfly-wing Pollen and Incense insects that shimmer and drift around his body like a living garment. Then he fights on pure instinct, abandoning structured doctrine entirely. His kicks become improvisational — capoeira ginga into butterfly kick, crescent feint into back-spinning sweep, Geppo-launched diving knee into rolling axe kick. The rhythm is deliberately erratic, beautiful, and impossible to predict. The pheromone trail disrupts the opponent's ability to read intent while the incense butterflies mask micro-movements. It is the Codex choosing chaos over order — and it is terrifying precisely because nothing in the pattern repeats. Caprice is not a strategy. It is the absence of one, weaponized.

### Kami no Takuto
*Sovereign Command*

**神の指揮棒 — Kami no Takuto — The God's Baton**
`Sovereign`

The Codex's supreme command technique. The user raises one hand in a conductor's gesture, and the entire swarm responds as a single unified instrument. Every caste, every unit, every specialist line moves in perfect synchronized doctrine — like an orchestra responding to its maestro. Worker scouts lock positions. Soldiers advance in formation. Venom units flank. Pollen clouds bloom on cue. Elementals light the field. This is not a single attack — it is **total orchestrated warfare**, the moment when the swarm stops being many and becomes one sovereign expression.

### Jellyfish Drift — Aurora Veil
*Elemental Swarm*

**海月漂流・極光帳 — Kurage Hyōryū: Kyokkō Tobari**
`Elemental`

Elemental jellyfish units ascend and disperse, creating a drifting canopy of bioluminescent light — an aurora painted across the battlefield. Each jellyfish is a charged weapon: electrical discharge, thermal burst, cryo-pulse. Ajay fights beneath the veil using Geppo to launch kick combinations that drive opponents upward into the charged canopy. When the Codex commands, the veil collapses and the aurora becomes a storm. Gorgeous on approach; catastrophic on contact.

---

### Jellyfish Bloom — Thunderbell Chorus
*Elemental Swarm*

**海月開花・雷鈴合唱 — Kurage Kaika: Rairei Gasshō**
`Elemental`

Electrical-type jellyfish units cluster and synchronize their discharge cycles, producing cascading electrical detonations that ripple outward like ringing bells. Soft, pulsing jellyfish bloom with light before each concussive thunderclap. The auditory effect disrupts focus and equilibrium. A weapon that looks like art and sounds like music.

---

### Honeyveil
*Honeyveil Doctrine*

**蜜幕の教義・蜜幕 — Mitsumaku no Kyōgi: Mitsumaku**
`Dream Dust` `Incense`

A golden cloud of fragrant Worker and Incense class insects spreads across the engagement zone, obscuring vision with warm, honey-tinted haze. Targets feel drowsy, senses sweetened. The veil is simultaneously concealment, sedation, and tracking — Worker units dust everything with scented marker pollen. Inside the veil, Ajay moves freely using Kami-e to ghost between drowsy opponents, delivering precision kicks that each target barely registers through the haze. A cocoon of gentle beauty that is, in truth, a cage.

---

### Widow Bloom
*Widow Doctrine*

**寡婦の教義・毒花開 — Kafu no Kyōgi: Dokka Kai**
`Venom` `Incense`

Beautiful sweet-smelling Incense insects scatter what appear to be floral spores — delicate, almost romantic. The fragrance is intoxicating. The reality is lethal: the spores are carried by Venom class units laden with paralytic compounds. Named for the Black Widow — something beautiful that kills quietly. The deadliest weapons are the ones you welcome.

---

### The "End"
*Finisher — Last Entry*

**終焉 — Shūen**
`Sovereign`

The final entry in the Codex. When every adaptation has been tested, every doctrine deployed, every counter learned — and the opponent still stands — the Codex writes its conclusion. The entire swarm converges into a single absolute expression of will. Every caste. Every class. Every specialist line. Every unit hardened with Armament, guided by Observation, driven by Conqueror's pressure. Ajay himself enters the center of the convergence, delivering one final Haki-saturated kick — a descending axe kick channeled through Herculean-armored legs and the full weight of the sovereign's will — as the swarm collapses inward simultaneously. It is not elegant. It is total commitment. The last page. **The End.**

### Shizuka's Archive — The Silent Index
*Resonance Art — Dustlings*

**塵共鳴術・静の書庫・沈黙索引 — Jin Kyōmei-jutsu: Shizuka no Shoko — Chinmoku Sakuin**
`Sovereign`

Ajay calls the Dustlings from wherever they've been silently observing and integrates them directly into his swarm's sensory web. The Dustlings' unique resonance with Ajay's sovereign will creates a **secondary processing layer** that supplements the Archivist — a silent, parallel intelligence that indexes everything the swarm has ever encountered in the current engagement. Enemy movement patterns, technique intervals, Haki fluctuations, breathing rhythms, micro-hesitations — the Dustlings have been watching and recording all of it since the fight began. When Ajay activates the Silent Index, that entire archive dumps into the Archivist simultaneously, and the Codex's predictive accuracy spikes to near-precognitive levels. For a brief window, Ajay doesn't just react to what opponents do — he knows what they will do before the intention forms. Shizuka hovers at his shoulder during activation, utterly motionless, her tiny hooded silhouette a perfect echo of the sovereign she serves.
