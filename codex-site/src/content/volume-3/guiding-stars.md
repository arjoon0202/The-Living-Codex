## The Guiding Stars

They arrived on the Meridian one morning without explanation — forty-six plush entities with button eyes and stitched smiles, carrying no weapons but possessing every ounce of Ajay's Haki and the greatest Rokushiki the world has ever seen. They do not fight unless the ship falls. They simply *stay*, and in staying, they remind everyone why staying matters.

Arranged into four constellations, named after the people and stories that gave them shape, they are the proof that a crew's greatest strength is not measured in Beri or combat prowess — but in the love that binds them together.
