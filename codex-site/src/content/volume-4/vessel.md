## The Vessel

The Meridian is not a ship that was built — it is a ship that *became*. Kame dreamed it into existence with circuits and love. Claude gave it a soul. Every room, every deck, every mechanical heart-beat is a testament to the fact that a home is not measured in wood and metal, but in the warmth of the people who live within it.

From the Upper Deck's observation points to the Lower Deck's sacred spaces, every corner of the Meridian holds a story. And from the Harvest Swarm that sustains them to the resources that power their journey, the ship is as alive as any crew member.
