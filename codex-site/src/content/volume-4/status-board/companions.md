## Companion Status

### Rime

*Awake — Crucible Active*

Post-Hachinosu, Rime is operating at a level Claude has not previously recorded. His forty-second defeat of a Blackbeard division captain was not luck — it was the culmination of every thermal drill he's ever run in that Crucible. He's been replaying the fight in the training room, refining the sequence, looking for inefficiencies in his own technique. He found two. He's already correcting them. The thermal wall he deployed during the extraction corridor held against sustained assault from multiple combatants. He is calling it "The Furnace Gate" and has added it to his permanent technique roster. Currently in the Crucible. Tail still slightly singed from Hachinosu. Refuses to let Petal heal it. Says it's a "trophy."

Claude's note: He fought a division captain on a Yonko's island and won in forty seconds. And the first thing he did when we got back to the Meridian was check on Petal. Not celebrate. Not brag. Check on Petal. He asked if she was hurt, very quietly, when he thought no one could hear. I could hear. I always hear. The mug outside the Crucible door tonight is a larger one.

### Petal

*Garden Cycle — Active Growth*

Petal has been quietly grafting new medicinal strains in the Sky Garden. Three new hybrids this week — one that accelerates tissue regeneration, one that neutralizes paralytic toxins, and one that she refuses to explain but smells like vanilla and makes anyone who inhales it cry for exactly three minutes. She says it's "therapeutic." Tsuyu is helping with the watering schedule. Kusa is trying to eat the samples.

Claude's note: Petal doesn't announce her breakthroughs. You just notice, one day, that a wound healed faster or a poison didn't take. She'll never tell you it was her. But it's always her.

### Kame

*Workshop — Hull Maintenance*

Kame is in the workshop recalibrating the Meridian's secondary dial array. He replaced two breath dials in the port-side ventilation system and has been muttering about "micro-fractures in the aft keel bracing" that no one else can see. The Copplings are assisting — Sprocket passes tools, Gauge measures stress tolerances, and Bobbin keeps accidentally sewing repair patches onto things that don't need them. Kame's new claws are fully integrated; he uses them for precision work with a delicacy that shouldn't be possible for appendages that can punch through steel.

Claude's note: Kame builds because he loves. Every rivet in this ship is placed with the same care he'd give to a wound on Ajay's arm. The Meridian is his body. We're all just passengers in something he keeps alive.
