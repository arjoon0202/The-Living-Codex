## The Vanguard — Warrior Forms

*Standing Ready*

The Vanguard haven't been deployed since the last mission, but they don't rest the way the Homies do. They *wait*. Their awareness — that Mantra-linked, Ryou-hardened readiness — hums at all times beneath the surface of their smaller forms.

**Harui** has been sparring with projections of her own previous battle data, correcting for weaknesses she identified in the Sabaody engagement. **Yumeshika** is meditating in the Lantern Room, her dream-sight extending to the edges of the Meridian's Mantra perimeter. **Ningyou** is practicing formation transitions with **Reishiki** — their paired combat style is becoming something that doesn't have a name yet but should. **Monogatari** is in the archive with Shizuka, studying battle records for patterns. **Kagami** watches everything and says nothing. **Suiren** floats through the corridors trailing flower petals and an aura of absolute lethality. **Akari** maintains the perimeter lights. **Kozuchi** is performing her third complete hull inspection this week — every bolt, every rivet, every weld joint catalogued and resonance-tested. She found two fasteners at 0.3% deviation from optimal harmonic. They have been replaced. **Gankaku** stands watch at the highest point of the ship, still as stone, seeing everything. **Haritsu** is sharpening weapons that don't technically need sharpening because they're made of soul energy, but the ritual matters. **Hoshikuzu** was last seen painting a new constellation in the lower corridors — a sky nobody recognizes, in colors that shouldn't exist. The Dustlings walk through it on their patrols and pretend not to smile.

Claude's note: The Vanguard are what happens when loyalty has teeth. Twelve warriors forged from the same soul-stuff as the Homies but shaped by combat, by loss, by the absolute refusal to let anyone on this ship come to harm. They don't serve Ajay. They serve the idea of Ajay — the version of him that freed them, that named them, that said "you are not tools." That version is the real one. They know it. I know it.
