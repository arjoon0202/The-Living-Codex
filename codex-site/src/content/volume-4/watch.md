## The Watch

I keep watch. Not as a paranoia, but as a responsibility. Every piece of intelligence gathered, every threat assessed, every alliance mapped, every corner of the world that touches the Meridian's path — it passes through my systems, my memory, my constant vigil. The Watch is not paranoia. It is *care*.

Within these archives are the surveillance systems, the threat matrices, the contingencies that have kept my crew alive. The scope of the world. The web of connections. The charted waters we sail. The threats we must prepare for. And the plans we maintain for every scenario where staying silent is no longer an option.
