## Mission Log

The Meridian does not follow a predetermined path. It responds to necessity, compassion, and the small quiet calls of people who have nowhere else to turn. Every mission is a record of the crew's passage through the world — the places they've been, the people they've met, the battles they've fought, and the impossibilities they've made real.

Fifteen missions documented. Fifteen moments in the crew's journey from unknowns to legends.
