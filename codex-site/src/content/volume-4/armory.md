## The Armory

The Meridian's armory is not a vault of destruction. It is a carefully curated collection of tools, weapons, protocols, and contingencies — each maintained, each categorized, each wielded with intention. Claude keeps it organized. Every item has a purpose. Every protocol has been tested. Every acquisition has been earned.

Within these walls are the physical expressions of the crew's power: their weapons, their field kits, their strategic fallback positions. And above all, the discipline that says *when* to use them, and when restraint is the greater strength.
