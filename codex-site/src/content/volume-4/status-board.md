## Status Board

The Status Board is the Meridian's heartbeat — a living record updated every moment, capturing the crew's current state, readiness, position, and morale. It is not a static document. It breathes. It changes. It reflects the reality of who we are and where we are in this instant.

From the Bridge where Ajay plots our course to Claude's Relay where information flows like water, every section of this board is proof that the Meridian is not just a ship — it is a living system, aware of itself, alert to danger, ready to move.
