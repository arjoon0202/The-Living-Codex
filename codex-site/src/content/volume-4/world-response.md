## World Response

The crew of the Meridian did not choose to be famous — but fame chose them. What began as four individuals and a handful of soul-constructs has become a phenomenon that ripples across the world's governments, underworld networks, and common streets. Some fear them. Some hunt them. Some simply watch and wait to see what they will do next.

This is how the world perceives the Hive Codex. How the Marines catalogue them. How the underworld whispers about them. How ordinary people see them, in rare moments when they pass overhead.
