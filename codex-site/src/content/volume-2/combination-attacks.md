## Combination Attacks

The Hive Codex crew does not fight as individuals. Every pairing, every formation, every synchronized strike is an extension of the collective bond that holds the Meridian together. When Ajay and Rime move in concert, it is not two bodies fighting as one — it is one person split across two expressions. When Petal's healing flows through a trio assault, the line between attack and support dissolves entirely.

These are the signature formations, the devastating pairings, the moments when the crew's greatest strength — the fact that they would die for each other — becomes weaponized into something the world has learned to fear.

- **Ajay Pairings** — Two-person combinations centered on the captain
